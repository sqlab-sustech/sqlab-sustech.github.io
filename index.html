About Us

Software Quality Lab @ SUSTech

SQLab (Software Quality Lab) is part of the Intelligent Software Engineering research group at the Southern University of Science and Technology. The lab is headed by Dr. Yepang Liu. We are interested in developing practical techniques to assure software quality and improve developers' productivity.
